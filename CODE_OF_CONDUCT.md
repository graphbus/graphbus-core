# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

GraphBus is a technical community. We want it to stay that way â€” focused, respectful, and welcoming to everyone who wants to build things.

We pledge to make participation in GraphBus a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, experience level, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Standards

**Positive behaviors:**
- Being respectful and constructive in code reviews and discussions
- Welcoming contributors of all skill levels
- Focusing on the technical merits of ideas
- Giving and gracefully accepting feedback
- Acknowledging when you're wrong and learning from it

**Unacceptable behaviors:**
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Trolling or deliberately inflammatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Any other conduct that a reasonable person would consider unprofessional

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

Instances of unacceptable behavior can be reported to [hello@graphbus.com](mailto:hello@graphbus.com). All complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
